About Me

I am a mission-driven front-end web developer specialized in JavaScript, React, and Node.js/Express.js. My path has taken me from Germany to Israel and from TV journalism to software development. As a passionate long-distance runner, I know that sometimes in life you simply have to grit your teeth.
